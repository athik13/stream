Update instructions from version 1.5 to 2.0 -
PLEASE READ AND FOLLOW THE STEPS CAREFULLY.
Before starting, keep a backup of files and database of your running 1.5 version. So that there will be no fear of losing data.

1. Open the update_pack folder. Copy the assets folder from here and replace your server's assets folder.
	Folder location: Your application root folder/assets

2. Copy the controllers folder from here and replace your server's controllers folder.
	Folder location: Your application root folder/application/controllers

3. Copy the helpers folder from here and replace your server's helpers folder.
	Folder location: Your application root folder/application/helpers

4. Copy the models folder from here and replace your server's models folder.
	Folder location: Your application root folder/application/models

5. Copy the views folder from here and replace your server's views folder.
	Folder location: Your application root folder/application/views

6. Import the update.sql file into your database.

You have successfully updated your application. After that, Login as Admin.

The latest version of 2.0. If you face any kind of difficulties, let us know through tickets.
That's it.
